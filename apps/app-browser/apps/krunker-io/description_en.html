Krunker.io is a multiplayer first-person shooter video game. The goal of the game is to gain points by eliminating other players or completing objective of different game modes, and by having the most points, win the game. There are several game modes, such as Free-For-All and Team Deathmatch. Players can choose from several different classes with different weapons, such as the Triggerman (AK-47), Hunter (AWP), and Detective (Revolver). The games take place in different maps.
